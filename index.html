Why FamilySmart?

For families of children or youth who are struggling with their mental health, services can be difficult to understand. Who better to help navigate these systems than those who’ve been there?

FamilySmart’s parent peer support workers have a powerful combination of lived experience, thorough training and support. Working alongside healthcare professionals, they provide families with context, clarity, and caring.

That’s because FamilySmart has the most experience, expertise, and longevity of any comparable program. Our parent peer support workers are uniquely equipped to complement the work of healthcare professionals.

They’re a true asset to any clinical team. After all, both is best.

Why peer-to-peer support?

Reduces hospitalization, symptom distress, isolation, and stigma

Improves coping skills, access to resources, and quality of life

Source: The Mental Health Commission of Canada
